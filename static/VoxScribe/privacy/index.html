Privacy Policy

VoxScribe Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 7, 2026

Summary

VoxScribe is a desktop application for transcription and qualitative analysis. The app does not collect personal data for tracking, advertising, or analytics.

Audio And Transcript Files

Files you import, transcripts you create, codes, memos, themes, and exported project data remain under your control on your device or wherever you choose to save them.

Model Downloads

If a selected transcription model is not already available locally, VoxScribe may download model files from the model provider so transcription can run.

No Tracking

VoxScribe does not use third-party advertising trackers, does not sell user data, and does not collect analytics through the submitted app privacy manifest.
